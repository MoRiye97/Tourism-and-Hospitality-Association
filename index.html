About Us

Tourism & Hospitality Association!

The Tourism and Hospitality Association (THA), founded in 2022 is one of Limkokwing University of Creatity and Technology's first and largest student-run organisations. It represents,connects and engages everyday with an inspiring and engaging network of 137 both tourism and/or hospitality students from 5 Universities Nationally.
