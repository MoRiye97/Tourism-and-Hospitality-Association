The Tourism and Hospitality Association (THA), founded in 2022 is one of Limkokwing University of Creatity and Technology's first and largest student-run organisations. It represents,connects and engages everyday with an inspiring and engaging network of 137 both tourism and/or hospitality students from 5 Universities Nationally.

Our Standing Committees

THA has a dedicated organ which aims to implement an optimal learning environment for all Tourism & Hospitality around campus,the SCTS,the SCTP and the SCHS. Through all our joint efforts, we work to create sustainable changes around the country and industry for ourselves as tourism and hospitality students, for generations to come & for our future client (tourists) and our community who are in fact the final beneficiaries of our eductaion
The work of each standing committee is complemented by several activities such as, training, campaigns and advocacy efforts, offering members space to learn in both formal and informal settings.
Plenty of projects,activities,conferences & workshops are organised each year through member organisations within the THA Network, allowing students to become agents of change at home.
